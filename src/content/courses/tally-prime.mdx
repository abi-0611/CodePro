## Overview

Tally Prime is widely used for business accounting. This course teaches the practical skills needed for everyday work: setting up a company, creating masters, entering vouchers, understanding GST basics, and generating reports.

### What you’ll learn

- Set up companies, ledgers, and inventory masters
- Record sales, purchases, receipts, and payments
- Understand basic GST workflows and key reports
- Generate common accounting reports for review

### Why take this course

1. Practical, job-relevant accounting workflows
2. Guided practice and common scenario drills
3. Confidence with reports and reconciliation
